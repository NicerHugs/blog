Hi, I'm nicerhugs

I had been my whole life a bell, and never knew it until at that moment I was lifted and struck.
- Annie Dillard, Pilgrim at Tinker Creek

Hi, I'm nicerhugs. I'm a multi-potentialite. Which means I like to keep a variety of experiences, do a lot of different things, and learn basically as much as there is to learn. I also spend a lot of time (over) thinking about just about everything. Sometimes I document the things I do, but mostly I document the things I think. That's what this blog is all about.

If you're interested in my other endeavors, feel free to check out my other sites. My coding portfolio is rarely up to date, but it does exist. My older blogs are available for your scrutiny in the archive. And at some point I'll get some gumption and post things I've made.

Have a question for me? Want to tell me I'm crazy or need to prove me wrong? Just have a hankering for some chatter? I love pen pals and I love reasons to think about new stuff. Give me a good excuse to do some more writing. You can always reach me at nicerhugs@gmail.com.
